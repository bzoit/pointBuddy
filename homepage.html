What is PointBuddy?

PointBuddy is a web-app which allows Germantown Academy Lower School teachers to give points to 5th grade students. Students can later redeem points for special prizes. PointBuddy encourages students to actively uphold GA's core values and model positive behavior, both academically and virtuously.



What are the benefits of PointBuddy?

There are many benefits to using PointBuddy. One of the most important benefits of PointBuddy is that it encourages students to be virtuous, disciplined, resourceful, and productive. All of which are characteristics that can lead to future success throughout one's academic career. Teachers have the opportunity to reward students for positive behavior in the moment and in real-time. This practice yields the highest rate of positive behavior change among children.
